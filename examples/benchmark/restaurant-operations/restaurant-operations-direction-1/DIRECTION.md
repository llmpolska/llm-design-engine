# Creative direction

- id: restaurant-operations-direction-1
- name: Service / Tasks 1
- metaphor: service as a route through tasks
- visual narrative: Service enters as context, concentrates around coordinate, then resolves through an explicit next action. This direction gives service the first move and tasks the proof.
- composition: route
- typography: Use typographic contrast to mark confirm and let service set the reading pace.
- materials: marked service surfaces, service marks, coordinate surfaces, coordinate traces
- interaction: confirm changes emphasis in place while preserving the original route.
