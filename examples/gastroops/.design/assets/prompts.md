# GastroOps imagery prompts

These prompts are derived from the approved Professional Kitchen Control Room direction. They intentionally name the domain materials and reject generic AI imagery.

- **Hero material study:** Blackened steel worktop, printed kitchen tickets, station markings, warm pass lighting, scratched stainless edges, one clear handoff ticket in focus. Negative constraints: stock photography, generic dashboard, glowing gradients, glass panels, robots, abstract blobs. Aspect ratio: 16:10. Placement: hero working surface.
- **Blueprint route:** A restaurant service pass drawn as a precise annotated route from prep to handoff to close, thermal-paper texture and coral exception marks. Negative constraints: generic flowchart, device mockup, unrelated abstract shapes. Aspect ratio: 4:3. Placement: process section.
- **Station study:** Close crop of temperature log, knife-safe station marking, ticket rail, and a mineral teal verified stamp; honest directional light. Negative constraints: staged startup desk, random stock imagery, neon glow. Aspect ratio: 3:2. Placement: evidence section.
- **Social preview:** Editorial title plate reading “GastroOps / Professional Kitchen Control Room” with steel, ticket paper, pass amber, and a coral route line. Negative constraints: SaaS landing page screenshot, purple cloud, robot head. Aspect ratio: 1.91:1. Placement: Open Graph image.
