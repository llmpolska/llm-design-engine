# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We want LLM Design Engine to be a useful, rigorous, and welcoming open-source project.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, specific, curious, and willing to revise an idea when evidence changes. Review the artifact and its behavior, not the person. Make room for different domains, cultures, abilities, and creative references.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, doxxing, sexualized conduct, threats, or deliberate disruption are not acceptable. Do not submit content that imitates a living creator or appropriates a community's cultural material without care and context.

## Enforcement

Report a concern through a private maintainer contact on the [LLMPolska GitHub organization](https://github.com/llmpolska) or open a clearly labelled issue when privacy is not required. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this policy.
